Generic Comfort Uno

Generic Comfort Uno - ergonomic computer desk, wich allows users change position. Now You can work at PC sitting, standing up or walking on a treadmill.



Work standing up benefits:

Less back pain: Sitting for long periods of time tightens your muscles and can hurt your lower back, especially if you have bad posture. Standing desks seem to help ease back pain, but doctors don't know how much time you need to stand to get this benefit.

More calories burned: One study showed that standing sheds 88 calories an hour, compared to 80 calories for sitting. Walking burns a lot more -- 210 calories an hour.



Work sitting:

The classic work pose of an office worker. But the correct landing at the computer is possible only with a well-thought-out workplace. The desk and chair must be ergonomic. Keep your arms relaxed and your elbows completely flat on the table.



Walking:

The combination of a table and a treadmill can often have a positive effect on work results. Partial switching to physical activity allows a person to reduce the level of stress, "relax mind", and find a non-standard solution for the problem. It is not for nothing that in thoughtfulness, many of us prefer not to sit still, but to walk. And, of course, daily activities will allow you to take care of your health, reduce the risk of diabetes, obesity, stroke and many other problems associated with physical inactivity.



Laying position:

You can choose laying position, if you just read the news, check your mail or do other business that does not require active use of the keyboard and mouse.

How to change height:

The table is electrically adjustable in height, allowing you to adjust to your height quickly and quietly.
